Strawberry shortcake Recipe

This treat is very a tasty desert that could serve and satisfy 5 whole persons!

Ingridients

    Sugar
    Flour
    Eggs
    Strawberries
    Milk

Steps

    First, you want to crack two eggs, pour 1 cup of sugar , 1 cup of flour and 15 ml of milk into abowl
    Next , you want to stir them up in pot
    Then, you lay the foundation into a pan so it could bake Next
    Finally, you want to bake the foundation 450 degrees in the oven for 35 minutes
